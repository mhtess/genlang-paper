In this experiment, we are interested in how common people believe certain actions are (for example: brushing one's teeth). You will be asked to provide your best guess as to the percentage of the US population that has done the action before. You will also be asked to rate how frequently a typical person does this action (for example: you might say the typical person brushes her teeth 2 times per day).

Please note: You will be asked to choose the appropriate time window (for example: per day). If the action is extremely rare (for example, jumping out of a plane), you can just report the total number of times that a typical person will do this in their life, by choosing the per life time rate.
